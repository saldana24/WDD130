Content

Home page

The best way to grow a business is with technology to help manage the way you get reviews from customers. Thousands of customers are satisfied with local businesses but they either don’t know how to review or it is too much of a hassle for them to find the way to make that review. With our software we will be able to make it easy for you to reach customers and show them an easy path to make that review The tools are easy to use so that we can provide customers an easy experience and will help get more customers. This way you can focus on what matters which is expanding your business and keep expanding.

With recent events and the way technology and online interaction is exponentially growing there is a need to have an online presence for everyone to be able to view it. Every person nowadays looks up reviews on a business before going to a store, so the more reviews but more importantly good quality reviews you have you will get more traffic to your website and you business

Why will people come to your site? They will come because no one wants to handle work that can be automated saving owners time and headaches. What questions will your visitors expect your site to answer for them? Can I have a demo of the software? What platforms can it interact with? Do i need to be techsavy to use this? What types of activities will visitors want to complete on your site? Set up a demo with our people, see prices, see features What information is necessary to complete any actions your site offers? Email, phone number to set up a meeting. After that you will need company name, create an account with owner’s info and a form of payment for monthly payment. Why will your visitors come to your site instead of another to get this information or complete the actions? Been a new company we will offer competitive rates for trying us, we will of course strive to offer the best customer service that can’t be provided at other big companies.

Reviews impact how much business you get Business owners like you are getting up to speed with the importance of reviews: -75 percent of businesses say that online reviews and comments are important to their sales and reputation. -17 percent say that reviews can “make or break” a business. -Just over a third say that reviews are becoming even more important. Reviews really matter to customers as well: -Nine in ten consumers who look for reviews are hesitant to purchase if there are no reviews for a business. -On average, a customer reads ten reviews before trusting a business. -Between 70 and 95 percent of consumers say that online reviews influence who they choose to buy from. -Almost three quarters of consumers trust the comments in a review more than the star rating.
